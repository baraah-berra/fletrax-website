Fletrax: Your Vehicle Tracking and Monitoring Solution

At Fletrax, We provide an advanced and user-friendly platform designed specifically for the unique needs of car rental businesses.

Get Started

Our Services:

  • 1. Real-Time Vehicle Tracking: Keep tabs on your entire fleet in real-time. Our cutting-edge GPS tracking technology allows you to monitor the location and status of each vehicle, ensuring that you always know where your assets are.
  • 2. Geofencing: Set up geographical boundaries and receive instant alerts if any vehicle enters or exits these predefined areas. This feature adds an extra layer of security and helps prevent unauthorized use.
  • 3. Driver Behavior Analysis: Promote safer driving habits with our driver behavior analysis tools. Monitor speed, acceleration, and braking patterns to encourage responsible driving practices, ultimately reducing the risk of accidents and optimizing fuel efficiency.
  • 4. Customizable Reports: Generate detailed reports tailored to your specific needs. From mileage tracking to fuel consumption, our customizable reports provide the insights you need to make informed decisions.
